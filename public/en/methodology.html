Methodology

Why built this

The AI space is flooded with secondary commentary and hype. This project is designed to provide purely objective, primary-source information through automation.

Who is it for

If you value objectivity and facts over opinions, want to know "what happened" instead of "what it means", and expect all sources to be traceable, this digest is for you.

Information Sources

We monitor 25 high-quality, primary tech sources including OpenAI and Anthropic blogs, MIT Tech Review, Hacker News, and industry newsletters.

Built with Python, Jinja2, and Google Gemini 2.5 Flash.
